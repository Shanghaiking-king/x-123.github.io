Bio

I am an associate professor leading a computer vision group at SUES and the deputy director for industrialization of AI models in Anhui Province, China. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on endowing robots with visual perception and recognition abilities. I have received awards for contributions to video analysis and intelligent aerospace and serve as an associate editor for Applied Intelligence since 2014. Currently, I explore the capabilities of foundation models in downstream tasks. I enjoy identifying practical challenges and crafting solutions grounded in domain knowledge. I have published over 60 papers in top journals and conferences and have led projects in robot vision, object re-identification, and medical image segmentation.
